# User Data Deletion Instructions for ShortSync

This document explains how users can request deletion of their data from ShortSync in compliance with Meta's Platform Policies.

## How to Request Data Deletion

Users can request deletion of their data through the following methods:

### Method 1: Email Request
Send an email to: **abdullahwarraich382@gmail.com**

Include in your email:
- Subject line: "Data Deletion Request - ShortSync"
- Your Facebook/Instagram account email or username
- Confirmation that you want all your data deleted

### Method 2: In-App Settings
1. Open ShortSync application
2. Navigate to Settings → Account  
3. Click "Disconnect All Accounts"
4. Confirm deletion of all associated data

### Method 3: Direct Account Disconnection
1. Go to your Facebook Settings
2. Navigate to Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites
3. Find "ShortSync" and click "Remove"
4. This will revoke our access and trigger automatic data deletion

## What Data Gets Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will permanently remove:

- ✅ All access tokens and credentials
- ✅ Uploaded video files and content
- ✅ Scheduled posts and posting history  
- ✅ User preferences and settings
- ✅ Analytics data associated with your account
- ✅ Any cached or temporary files

## Data Deletion Timeline

- **Immediate**: Access tokens and credentials are revoked immediately
- **Within 24 hours**: All user content (videos, posts) is deleted from our servers
- **Within 30 days**: All associated data, logs, and backups are permanently removed

## Verification

Once the deletion is complete (within 30 days), we will send a confirmation email to the address you provided in your deletion request.

## What We Cannot Delete

Please note: Once content is posted to Facebook, Instagram, or other social media platforms, it exists on those platforms independently. ShortSync can only delete data from our own servers. To remove content from social media platforms, you must delete it directly from those platforms.
