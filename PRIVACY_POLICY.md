# Privacy Policy for ShortSync

**Last Updated:** December 26, 2024

## Introduction

ShortSync ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal automation tool for managing and scheduling social media content across multiple platforms. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Facebook/Instagram Account Access**: We request permission to access your Facebook Page and Instagram Business account to post content on your behalf
- **Social Media Credentials**: Access tokens for Facebook, Instagram, and other connected platforms
- **Content Data**: Videos, captions, and scheduling information you upload to the application

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you use the application (e.g., posting frequency, platform preferences)
- **Technical Data**: Browser type, device information, and access times

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Post content to your connected social media accounts as scheduled
- Generate AI-powered descriptions and captions for your content
- Provide analytics on your posting activity
- Improve and optimize our service

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored locally on your device and our secure servers
- We use industry-standard encryption to protect your access tokens
- Videos and content are temporarily stored only for the duration needed to post to your social media accounts
- We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties

## Third-Party Services

ShortSync integrates with:
- **Facebook/Instagram Graph API**: To post content to your accounts
- **Google Gemini AI**: To generate content descriptions (optional)
- **Meta Platform Services**: For authentication and content publishing

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle user data.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access, update, or delete your account information at any time
- Revoke our access to your social media accounts
- Request a copy of your data
- Delete all your content from our servers

## Data Retention

- User content is deleted after successful posting to social media platforms
- Account credentials can be removed by disconnecting your social media accounts
- Analytics data may be retained for service improvement purposes

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
